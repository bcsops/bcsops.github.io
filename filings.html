Listings Available

All legally mandated documentation will be posted here for court purposes

The orders on this webpage will be listed as:
Year/Month/Day/Product Name.DocumentType - Title of Order

For example, a legal filing made to the courts would be:
2024/Septemer/16/Legal.CV03985532 - Legal Filing CV03985532

These are the keys used for document types

  • Legal - Legal filings made by Bylthe Carnegie Stone
  • FinancialReporting - Financial Reporting obligations
  • EarningReports - Mandated earnings reports for shareholders
  • Misc - Miscellaneous filings
  • Disclosure - Disclosures made about any product or operation
