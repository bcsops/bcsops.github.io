SECTION 1 - DEFINITIONS

This Financial Product has been approved by the Union of Columbia Federal Government ("Federal Government") as a NON risk-free financial instrument that is permitted for sale to INSTITUTIONAL clients. This Product may only be marketed to institutional clients due to the complex and risky nature of the Product.

The "Reference Entity" is defined as the company, sovereign, or other entity whose credit risk is being transferred under this agreement. It is the entity whose default or other credit events trigger a payout under the CDS.

A "Credit Event" A predefined event that affects the creditworthiness of the Reference Entity, including but not limited to:

  • Bankruptcy
  • Failure to Pay
  • Restructuring
  • Obligation Acceleration
Occurrence of a Credit Event leads to the Buyer’s right to receive compensation from the Seller.
