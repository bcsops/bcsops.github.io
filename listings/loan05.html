Recitals

The Lender agrees to provide the Borrower with a loan under the terms specified in this Agreement, and the Borrower agrees to repay the loan according to the repayment schedule. This Agreement includes provisions for asset seizure upon default.

1. Loan Terms

1.1 Loan Amount:

The Lender agrees to lend the Borrower the amount of $50000 (Fifty Thousand Union of Columbia Dollars) ("Loan Principal").

1.2 Interest Rate:

The loan shall bear interest at a rate of 8.0% across loan term, calculated from the date of disbursement until fully repaid.

1.3 Loan Disbursement Date:

The loan will be disbursed on 05/10/2024.

1.4 Loan Term:

The term of this loan is 10 (TEN) DAYS from the date of disbursement.

1.5 Repayment Schedule:

The Borrower agrees to repay the Loan Principal, plus interest, in ONE equal installments of $54000 (Fifty Four Thousand Union of Columbia Dollars), due on the 15th of October 2024.

2. Collateral and Asset Seizure

2.1 Collateral Pledged:

As security for the loan, the Borrower agrees to pledge the following assets ("Collateral") to the Lender:

  • Description of Asset 1: ALL ASSETS BELONGING TO NAMED BORROWER
  • Description of Asset 2 (if applicable): NOT APPLICABLE

The Borrower acknowledges that the Collateral will be used to secure the repayment of the loan. The Lender has the right to place a lien or any other security interest on the Collateral until the loan is fully repaid.

2.2 Rights Upon Default:

If the Borrower defaults on the loan as described in Section 4 of this Agreement, the Lender has the legal right to seize and liquidate the Collateral to recover any outstanding amounts owed, including principal, interest, fees, and expenses.

3. Loan Repayment

3.1 Payment Method:

Payments shall be made via CASH transfer to authorised intermediaries of Blythe Carnegie Stone

3.2 Early Repayment:

The Borrower may repay the loan in full at any time without penalty.

3.3 Late Payment:

In the event of late payment, the Borrower agrees to pay a late fee of $1000 for each day the payment is past due.

4. Default

4.1 Events of Default:

The Borrower shall be in default under this Agreement if any of the following occurs:

  • Failure to make any payment when due under this Agreement.
  • Failure to maintain the Collateral as outlined in Section 5.
  • Bankruptcy or insolvency of the Borrower.

4.2 Notice of Default:

In the event of a default, the Lender shall provide written notice to the Borrower. The Borrower shall have 1 (ONE) day(s) from the date of the notice to cure the default by making full payment of any amounts due, including any late fees.

4.3 Seizure of Collateral:

If the Borrower fails to cure the default within the designated period, the Lender has the right to take possession of the Collateral without further notice to the Borrower. The Borrower agrees to cooperate with the Lender in facilitating the seizure of the Collateral.

5. Collateral Maintenance

5.1 Condition of Collateral:

The Borrower agrees to maintain the Collateral in good working order and to insure the Collateral against loss, theft, or damage for the duration of the loan.

5.2 Transfer of Title:

The Borrower agrees that, in the event of default and seizure of the Collateral, all rights to the Collateral shall be transferred to the Lender without any further action required by the Borrower.

6. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

6.1 Governing Law:

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Mayflower and the Union of Columbia.

6.2 Jurisdiction:

Any disputes arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts located in Clark County, Mayflower.

7. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes any prior discussions, agreements, or understandings of any kind.

8. Amendment

This Agreement may only be amended in writing and signed by both the Lender and the Borrower.

9. Severability

If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, such provision shall be severed, and the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.
